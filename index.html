Origin Story

Wizard Boy To Stick Wizard

As a child, the future legend was known as Wizard Boy. During his childhood, a demon possessed his best friend Derek and turned him into the hand that struck down Destralea, Wizard Boy's girlfriend.

Wizard Boy rushed in to save her, but Derek's blow hurled him into a parallel dimension. He grows into Stick Wizard by crossing dungeon after dungeon, gathering spells, and helping versions of himself in each elemental dimension until he can find the way home.
